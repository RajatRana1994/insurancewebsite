Google Ads

Don’t buy leads, generate your own.

There are 2 main ways to get leads for your business. The first is purchasing leads and the second is generating leads yourself. This second method is called organic lead generation. Organic lead generation is going to give you the highest ROI as it helps establish the strong online presence that you need.

Why use Google Ads?

Google Ads leads convert 50% better than purchased leads which will help you rise above your local competition.

Who will see your ads?

83% of U.S. shoppers who visited a store in the last week say they used online search before going into a store. Make sure your business is the first one they see.

What will your reach be?

Google owns 71% of the internet search market which means your ads will reach an ever growing audience.

Increase your organic lead flow.

Our goal is to get your agency in front of as many people as possible and the best way to do that is through Google Ads. We’ll use keywords to increase the amount of leads you’re generating per day. All leads will be sent directly to you in real time for optimal conversion rates.

Geo + keyword targeting = conversion.

We’ll use geo targeting to identify your highest converting zip codes and target your ads for higher conversion. Keyword targeting allows us to run your ads in front of searchers with high buying intent. For example, someone who searches “auto insurance quotes near me” is a buyer ready to talk to an expert and make a decision.

Custom ad creation and monitoring.

We’ll use tried and tested ad content that produces results. All content is customized for your business. Over time, we’ll work to make sure your ads are continually optimized and monitored.
